Day 5: Developing Assessments & Assignments

Coming Up on Day 5

In an online class, students can gain a sense of agency in the course through the development of transparent, scaffolded assessments and assignments that focus on opportunities to demonstrate mastery and receive actionable feedback. Designing effective online assessments and assignments requires instructors to think critically about the affordances of the tools available to them for facilitation.

For today's assignment, you will apply the transparent assessment design methodology to your own assignments to scaffold activities so that students are explicitly told why they are doing a task, what they are expected to do, and how they will be graded.
