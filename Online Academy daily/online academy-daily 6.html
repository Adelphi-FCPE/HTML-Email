Day 6: Asynchronous Online Learning

Coming up on Day 6

Today you'll learn strategies to foster community engagement in an asynchronous learning environment online. You’ll apply the technique known as Community of Inquiry (CoI), which balances social presence, teaching presence, and cognitive presence. CoI ensures a meaningful and socially connected learning experience for students.

The assignment for day 6 will be to open the Google document in Submission | Day 6 Assignment: Engagement and Community Module Activity. Use the same assignment from Day 5 or choose a different one to explore ways to enhance student-to-student, instructor-to-student, and content-to-student engagement. Submit your module activity on Moodle and your Engagement Plan via the Google Assignments page.
