Coming up on Day 10

Congratulations on making it to the final day of Online Academy!

Course evaluation is crucial for ensuring alignment with goals and objectives. While often seen as a final step, it should be an ongoing process throughout the entire course lifecycle. An essential tool for online course evaluation is the Online Course Quality Review Rubric (OSCQR). This rubric comprises 9 sections and 50 criteria to assess course design quality.

In the Day 10 Assignment: Review a Module in a Course (OSCQR Rubric), you have the opportunity to work directly with the OSCQR by evaluating a module from your course (or the entire course). Perhaps, however, you would like to use the rubric to review a module from or the entire Online Academy. Your choice. Do both, if you wish.
