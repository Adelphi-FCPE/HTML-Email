While the content and assignment for Day 4 focuses on the differences between formative and summative evaluations and creating rubrics, we would like to challenge you to take the discussion within the context of AI (artificial intelligence). Think through how you might integrate AI within assignments but ho also, you may need to change rubrics to reflect the supported or forbidden use of AI. For example:

  • If you will allow students to use AI, then will you change expectations for the assignment itself? Should the assignment be free of all errors?
  • Will your rubric include standards for appropriate and skillful use of AI?
  • How will your grading or rubric reflect consequences for using AI if it is forbidden?
