Coming up on Day 2

In the Design Stage of the ADDIE Model, you'll organize and prepare for course development using tools and templates. Through Backward Design, you'll define learning outcomes, determine evidence of learning, plan activities, and select course materials.

To get started with Backward Design, we've provided resources, worksheets, rubrics, and templates to help you align your Course-Level Learning Objectives (CLOs) with Modular Learning Objectives (MLOs).

For Day 2, your assignment is to apply Backward Design by filling out a worksheet that will help you:

  • Craft MLOs for a chosen module in your course, as well as instructional materials, activities and assessments for this same module
  • Align your MLOs for each instructional material, activity, and assessment, as well as with your CLOs

Once you complete this worksheet, you will continue to work with this same module on Day 3: Backward Design | Time on Task II by incorporating time on task estimates- stay tuned!
