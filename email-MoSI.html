Dear Adelphi Faculty:

The (STEM)2 Network is hosting a Mobile Summer Institute on Scientific Teaching (MoSI) at Hofstra University on Monday, January 13 through Thursday, January 16.

All STEM faculty (including psychology, nursing, and health sciences) are welcome to participate in this engaging professional development opportunity.

Be part of an inspiring, hands-on learning opportunity that will transform your classroom approach with evidence-based practices tailored to higher-education STEM fields. Facilitated by national science education experts, participants will develop new and innovative classroom materials.

MoSIs address teaching and learning challenges unique to colleges and universities. During a MoSI, participants engage in interactive workshops about evidence-based teaching strategies and project-based group work to create effective teaching modules for their classrooms. Participants leave the MoSI with peer-reviewed teaching materials and the skills to:

  • implement evidence-based teaching in their classrooms,
  • evaluate themselves and their peers, and
  • continue developing as reflective practitioners.

The (STEM)2 Mission

The (STEM)2 Network is a collaborative initiative between two- and four-year institutions in Eastern Queens and Long Island. This was designed to transform and enhance undergraduate STEM education through equitable, student-centered systems.

This initiative focuses on the pivotal role of faculty in undergraduate STEM education while creating and strengthening pedagogical collaboration between institutions. By doing so, faculty can create change in and out of their classrooms.
